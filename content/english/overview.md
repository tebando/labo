## Education Data Utilization and Learning Design Lab

Our lab promotes practical research that can be applied in school settings, with three pillars: information education, elementary school programming education, and educational data science.

### Research Policy

- Use of educational data based on learning logs and lesson data for lesson improvement
- Practical learning design that connects goals, assessment, and activities
- AI literacy development based on ICT utilization, including generative AI

### Education and Research Collaboration

- Research supervision in the Graduate School of Education at Naruto University of Education
- Collaborative research linked to classroom practice in the field
- Empirical research through collaboration with educational institutions inside and outside the university

For individual faculty background, academic memberships, and awards, please refer to the personal bio on the members page.
